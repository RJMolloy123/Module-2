Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients Directions
1 cup milk Prepare all of your ingredients
4 tablespoons melted butter In a bread machine pan, place the milk, beaten egg, melted butter.
1 egg, beaten Add your water, vanilla pudding mix, bread flour, sugar, salt and yeast in the order recommended by the manufacturer.
4 tablespoons water When cycle is finished, remove the dough, and knead for 3 to 5 minutes.
4 cups bread flour Roll out to a large rectangle.
1/2 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix Mix together the softened butter, brown sugar and cinnamon.
1/2 teaspoon salt Spread over dough. Sprinkle with chopped walnuts and raisins, if desired.
2 1/4 teaspoons bread machine yeast Roll the dough into a log. Pinch to seal seams. Cut into 1/2 inch to 1 inch slices, and place in a greased 9x13 inch pan.
1 cup packed brown sugar Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Bake for 15 to 20 minutes.

Cornbread

Ingredients Directions
1 cup yellow cornmeal Position rack in center of oven; preheat to 400°F. Butter 8-inch square baking pan.
½ cup all purpose flour Add creamed corn, butter and egg.
3 tablespoons sugar Whisk cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in large bowl to blend.
2 teaspoons baking powder Stir just until blended. Spoon batter into pan.
1 large egg, beaten to blend Bake until edges begin to pull away from pan sides and tester inserted into center comes out clean, about 20 minutes.

Doughnuts

Ingredients Directions
3/4 cup vegetable oil Heat ½ cup of the oil in a medium skillet over medium-low heat.
1 8-count package large refrigerated biscuits (such as Pillsbury Grands) Place the biscuits on a cutting board. Using a 1-inch round cookie cutter or shot glass, cut a hole in the center of each biscuit, reserving the extra dough for "holes."
1/2 cup sugar Place 4 of the doughnuts and holes in the skillet and cook until golden brown, 1 to 1½ minutes per side. Transfer to a wire rack or paper towel–lined plate to drain. Add the remaining oil to the skillet, reheat, and cook the remaining doughnuts and holes.
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon n a large bowl, combine the sugar and cinnamon. Gently toss the warm doughnuts in the mixture a few at a time. Serve warm or at room temperature. This recipe makes 8 doughnuts, plus holes.

Breaded Chicken

Ingredients Directions
6 skinless, boneless chicken breasts Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
2 cups sour cream Rinse and trim the chicken breasts. Pat dry. Generously coat both sides of each breast with sour cream.
2 cups crushed cornflakes cereal Put crushed cornflake crumbs in a shallow plate or bowl and season to taste. Dip the sour cream coated chicken in the seasoned cornflake crumbs.
1 teaspoon Italian-style seasoning Place coated chicken in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
6 tablespoons butter, melted Drizzle with melted butter/margarine and bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour.
